Reimagining Remote Connectivity

This research dashboard explores the "Rodial" system, a novel architecture using a network's digital twin to provide full-strength, low-latency internet access to mobile devices, regardless of their proximity to the physical router. We introduce an ML model for intelligent network control and simulate its performance at scale.
